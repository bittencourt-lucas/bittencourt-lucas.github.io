Week Eight

Designing, Defining, and Triggering CSS3 Transitions without Custom Libraries (Thought Library)

Transitions have four properties that can be changed:

  • transition-property: Which CSS property will be affected.
  • transition-duration: How long the transition will last.
  • transition-timing-function: The timing function of the transition.
  • transition-delay: How long until the transition starts.

It's possible to edit more than one transition property at the same CSS line!

There are different timing functions. By the way, there's a 1 second delay before the transition starts, so hold your mouse hovering!
