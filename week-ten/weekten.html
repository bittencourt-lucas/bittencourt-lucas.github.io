Week Ten

Demonstrate fluency in Designing, Defining, and Triggering CSS3 Animations without Custom Libraries (Thought Library)

The building blocks of an Animation are the Keyframes and the Properties.

Keyframes are composed of three parts:

  • Name: The name of the Animation being created
  • Stage: The percentage state of the Animation
  • CSS Properties: The properties being altered

The Properties of the Animation are the following:

  • animation-name
  • animation-duration
  • animation-delay
  • animation-iteration-count
  • animation-direction
  • animation-timing-function
  • animation-fill-mode
